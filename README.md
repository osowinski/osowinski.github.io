# osowinski.github.io

Personal website that acts as a portfolio displaying my projects and resume.

I have coded and hosted my own website before, but unfortunately, I lost access to my old account and had to recreate everything from scratch. I took this as a chance to redo the site now that it’s been a few years and I've learned how to better style the front-end with HTML and CSS.

The website took around one week to build and deploy. I designed the initial skeleton and built around it, making adjustments and additions every day. It is a significant jump from what my original website looked like, as I utilized more advanced animations, focused on clean UI/UX and strong visual hierarchy.

I hope it tells my story and shows relevant skills to those who wish to see them.
